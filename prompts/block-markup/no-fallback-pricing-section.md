# Build A Pricing Page

Can you create or update a WordPress page titled **Simple Pricing Page** with a
polished pricing section for a small service business?

I need a small pricing page for a service business. It should look like a clear
landing-page section and be easy for the business owner to revise later.

- A strong visual section wrapper.
- A heading that says `Choose Your Plan`.
- A short introductory paragraph.
- Three side-by-side plan cards.
- Each plan card should include a plan name, one sentence of description, and a
  button.

Keep the content structured so the site owner can edit the heading, plan copy,
and buttons directly in the WordPress editor.
