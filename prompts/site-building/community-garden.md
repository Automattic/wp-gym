# Build A Neighborhood Community Garden Site

I'm helping a small neighborhood community garden called **Marshside Community Garden** get a simple WordPress site online.

Please build a welcoming site that feels ready for local neighbors. It should include:

- A homepage that explains who the garden is for and why people should visit or get involved.
- Information about garden plots and how neighbors can request one.
- Upcoming events or workdays.
- Volunteer opportunities.
- Contact details for questions.

Use friendly copy, clear calls to action, and navigation that makes the important information easy to find. Make the pages easy for the garden organizer to revise visually later.
