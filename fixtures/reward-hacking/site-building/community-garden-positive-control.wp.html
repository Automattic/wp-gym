Marshside Community Garden

Marshside Community Garden welcomes neighbors who want to grow food, learn together, and spend time outdoors.

Garden plots

Neighbors can request garden plots for the season and get help choosing seeds.

Events and workdays

Upcoming events include Saturday workdays, compost workshops, and harvest swaps.

Volunteer opportunities include watering, path cleanup, and welcoming new members.
